Widget for Mobile

They are a bit similar to desktop ones, but used on mobile devices.
Its objective is to provide interactive services and provide the user with visual information.



in some cases, widgets not only display information, but you
can also interact with them, so that you can perform various actions - for example, enable or disable options or
device settings - from the home screen. Many widgets are also customizable, which helps make it easier to give your
screen a unique look. And in case customization options aren't included, the vast majority of Android launchers
offer the ability to easily resize them.
