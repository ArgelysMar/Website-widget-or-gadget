Gadget or Widget

In computing, a widget is a small application
or program, usually presented in files or small files that are run by a widget
engine or Widget Engine.

Desktop widgets are also known as desktop gadgets, and they are
a new category of mini applications; designed to provide information
or improve an application or services of a computer or computer, or any
type of interaction through the World Wide Web, for example an extension
of some business application, that provides us with real-time information
on the status of the business or organization.
However, widgets can do whatever the imagination wants
and interact with services and information distributed
on the Internet.
